The Nanoplastic Threat

Nanoparticle Sources

Primary nanoplastics from manufacturing and secondary particles from plastic degradation in the environment.

Cellular Penetration

Nanoplastics cross biological barriers through various mechanisms including endocytosis and diffusion.

Oxidative Stress

Induction of reactive oxygen species leading to cellular damage and inflammatory responses.

Neurotoxicity

Potential to cross blood-brain barrier causing neurological damage and behavioral changes.
