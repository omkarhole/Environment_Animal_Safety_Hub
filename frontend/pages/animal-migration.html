Rebuilding the Paths

It's not too late to repair migration corridors. Here are proven solutions being implemented worldwide:

Wildlife Crossings

Specially designed overpasses and underpasses allow animals to safely cross roads and highways. These structures are landscaped to resemble natural habitats.

  • 85% reduction in animal-vehicle collisions
  • 50+ species documented using crossings
  • Genetic connectivity restored between populations
  • Cost-effective compared to accident cleanup

Protected Corridors

Legally protected strips of land that connect separate wildlife habitats, allowing safe passage and genetic exchange between populations.

  • Ancient routes preserved for future generations
  • Genetic diversity maintained across populations
  • Climate adaptation through range shifts
  • Ecosystem services preserved for human communities

Smart Infrastructure

Advanced animal detection systems, wildlife-friendly fencing, and seasonal road closures timed with migration patterns.

  • Real-time detection of crossing animals
  • Automated warnings for approaching drivers
  • Temporary closures during peak migration
  • Reduced speeds in critical crossing zones
