Understanding Soil Compaction Impacts

Heavy machinery in conservation areas compresses soil particles, reducing pore spaces that are essential for:

  • Soil aeration - Oxygen availability for roots and microorganisms
  • Water infiltration - Natural drainage and groundwater recharge
  • Root penetration - Plant growth and nutrient uptake
  • Invertebrate habitats - Earthworms, insects, and soil-dwelling organisms
